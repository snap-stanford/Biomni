# Task: Retrieve Gene Information

Retrieve detailed information about a specific gene.

## Input

A gene identifier (Gene Symbol, Ensembl ID, or other ID):
- Example: BRCA1 (Gene Symbol)
- Or: ENSG00000012048 (Ensembl ID)

## Requirements

1. Retrieve comprehensive gene information including:
   - Gene symbol and full name
   - Chromosomal location
   - Gene function and biological role
   - Associated diseases or conditions
   - Key pathways the gene is involved in
2. Provide a well-organized summary in markdown format

## Expected Output Format

Provide:
- Gene symbol and name
- Chromosomal location (chromosome, coordinates)
- Function and biological role
- Disease associations
- Pathway involvement
- Any other relevant information
